SPORTS

Sports and games are essential to enjoy overall health and well being. These are a good way to keep fit mentally as well as physically. Various kinds of sports and games are played in different parts of the world. Sports and games offer numerous advantages and are thus highly recommended for everyone to indulge in the same irrespective of their age.

Indoor games such as chess and carom board serve as a good exercise for our brain. They enhance our thinking capacity and analytical skills and sharpen our mind. It is suggested to inculcate the habit of following a sport or game in children from the very beginning. It is good for their growth and overall development.
